Yes !! The answer is Menonimee. But why?

According to latest county health rankings & roadmaps data from the University of Wisconsin Population Health Institute, Menominee,WI ranked the least healthiest county in the state of Wisconsin (Out of 72 counties)

On July 3, 1959, former Wisconsin Governor Gaylord Nelson, Menominee became 72nd county and youngest of WI. The Menominee means “Wild Rice People”. The Menominee Indian Tribe’s residency in Wisconsin, and parts of Michigan and Illinois, dates back 10,000 years. Years of persecution, war and land grab led to poverty !!

What factors drove Menominee to this situation ?

Menominee has the highest Native American Population population in Wisconsin.

Poverty has negative impact on Mental & Physical Health says Tribal Chairman Ronald Corn Sr.

COVID-19 and high unemployment rates driven Gini Index low. (0 refers to high inequality)

What's being done to improve the situation ?

Only 5% of the population has access to exercise opportunities compared to Wisconsin state average of 78%.Therefore, a new family and community center has been opened for public and health related coaches are recruited.

Menominee has about 27% of population with poor/fair health which almost 2 times of the state average of 15%. Therefore, Menominee government plans to expand the existing tribal clinic and extend help for the population, especially children.
